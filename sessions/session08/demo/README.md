> [!TIP] > **position** controls how elements are positioned on the page.
> Use `relative` to adjust an element relative to its normal position,
> `absolute` to position it within the nearest container with an established position,
> and `fixed` or `sticky` to create persistent or floating effects.
> Combine with `z-index` to manage the **visual depth** of elements.
